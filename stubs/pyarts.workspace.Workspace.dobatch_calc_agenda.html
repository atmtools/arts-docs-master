property Workspace.dobatch_calc_agenda

Calculations to perform for each batch case.

See also: DOBatchCalc()

Parameters:

  • radiance_field (Tensor5) – Radiance field. See radiance_field [OUT]

  • irradiance_field (Tensor4) – Irradiance field also known as flux density. See irradiance_field [OUT]

  • spectral_irradiance_field (Tensor5) – Spectral irradiance field. See spectral_irradiance_field [OUT]

  • ybatch_index (Index) – Index of batch case. See ybatch_index [IN]
