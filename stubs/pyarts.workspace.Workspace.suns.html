suns

property Workspace.suns

Array of Sun.

This variable describes a list of suns. Each sun is described by a struct with its spectrum, radius, distance from center of planet to center of sun, temperature (if possible), latitude in the sky of the planet, longitude in the sky of the planet and the type

Default value

[]
