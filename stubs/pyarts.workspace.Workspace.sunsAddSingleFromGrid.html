Extracts a sun spectrum from a field of such data and adds it to suns.

The method allows to obtain the sun spectrum by interpolation from a field of such data. The sun spectrum is expected to be stored as the irradiance at the suns photosphere.

Unit:

  • GriddedField2: [W m-2 Hz-1]

    • Vector f_grid [Hz]

    • Vector stokes_dim [1]

Dimensions: [f_grid, stokes_dim]

This method performs an interpolation onto the f_grid. The point of f_grid that are outside the data frequency grid are initialized according to planck‚Äôs law of the temperature variable. Hence, a temperature of 0 means 0s the edges of the f_grid.
