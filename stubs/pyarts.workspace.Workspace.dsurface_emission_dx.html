dsurface_emission_dx

property Workspace.dsurface_emission_dx

The derivative of surface_emission with respect to quantities listed in dsurface_names.

Usage: Used internally of radiative transfer methods

Dimensions: [dsurface_names][f_grid, stokes_dim]

Workspace methods that can generate dsurface_emission_dx

  • SurfaceBlackbody()

  • SurfaceDummy()

  • SurfaceFastem()

  • SurfaceFlatScalarReflectivity()

  • SurfaceTessem()

  • iySurfaceFlatRefractiveIndex()
