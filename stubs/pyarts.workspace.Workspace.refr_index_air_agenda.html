refr_index_air_agenda

property Workspace.refr_index_air_agenda

Calculation of the refractive index of air.

This agenda should calculate the summed refractive index for all relevant atmospheric constituents, with respect to both phase and group velocity.

The include file ‘agendas.arts’ predefines several agendas that may either be used directly, or serve as inspiration.

Parameters:

  • refr_index_air (Numeric) – Real part of the refractive index of air. See refr_index_air [OUT]

  • refr_index_air_group (Numeric) – Group index of refractivity. See refr_index_air_group [OUT]

  • rtp_pressure (Numeric) – Pressure at a radiative transfer point. See rtp_pressure [IN]

  • rtp_temperature (Numeric) – Temperature at a radiative transfer point. See rtp_temperature [IN]

  • rtp_vmr (Vector) – Absorption species abundances for radiative transfer calculations. See rtp_vmr [IN]

  • f_grid (Vector) – The frequency grid for monochromatic pencil beam calculations. See f_grid [IN]
