Author(s): Richard Larsson

Parameters:

  • propagation_path_atmospheric_point (ArrayOfAtmPoint, optional) – Atmospheric points along the propagation path. See propagation_path_atmospheric_point, defaults to self.propagation_path_atmospheric_point [OUT]
